About The Project

The Fastest Pakistani is a groundbreaking initiative that showcases the incredible speed and agility of Pakistan's top athletes. This project highlights the dedication, training, and natural talent that drives our nation's fastest runners, sprinters, and speed athletes to achieve excellence on both national and international stages.

Our comprehensive program covers various speed disciplines including track and field, cycling, swimming, and motorsports. We document the journeys of athletes who have broken barriers and set new standards for Pakistani sports.

Our Mission

To identify, support, and celebrate Pakistan's fastest athletes while inspiring the next generation to pursue excellence in speed sports. We believe that every Pakistani has the potential for greatness, and through proper training and support, we can produce world-class athletes.

Key Focus Areas

  • Track & Field: Sprint events, hurdles, and relay races
  • Swimming: Freestyle, butterfly, and individual medley
  • Cycling: Road racing, track cycling, and time trials
  • Motorsports: Formula racing and motorcycle racing
